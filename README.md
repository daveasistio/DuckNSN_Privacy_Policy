# Privacy Policy — DuckNSN

**Effective Date:** March 8, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

Sparky Group LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the DuckNSN mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use DuckNSN.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Onboarding preferences** — branch, role, and category selections you make during setup. Stored locally on your device.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data** — search queries, features used, and general app interactions. Used to improve the App.
- **Device information** — device type, operating system version, and app version.
- **Purchase data** — subscription status and transaction history, processed through Apple's App Store and managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card information.

### Information We Do Not Collect
- We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information unless you contact us directly.
- We do not collect location data.
- We do not sell your data to third parties.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and operate the DuckNSN App and its features
- Process and manage your subscription
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Respond to support requests

---

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Onboarding preferences and search history are stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. We do not have access to this data.
- NSN catalog queries are processed through our secure backend (Supabase). Search queries are not linked to individual users.
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data we do process, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

DuckNSN is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
